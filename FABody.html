The Great White Shark

The Great White Shark, also known as Carcharodon carcharias,
is a coastal shark that continues to be an apex predator,
having little to no predators. The Great White Shark preys
on animals such as various types of fish and sea birds.
Male Great White Sharks measure out at an average of 4.0 M
and usually between 680 and 1,800 kg.
