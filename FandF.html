A well known part of the Washington State Fauna includes the Killer Whale, one that sticks around in every season and are prominant in spring as well so it would be advised to go whale watching.



















A big part of the flora of Washington State includes the Viburnum opulus, a bush with tart berries that mainly bloom in spring and summer. Hiking on local trails will find you these. They are non-toxic and great for eating as well.
